Exploring IPL Dataset using Google Sheets

Goal:

The goal of the mini project is to showcase skills in Data Analysis using Google Sheets via different Charts like Pie Chart, Bar Chart, Scorecard Chart.
  • Workbook Creation: Created a seperate workbook to perform the analysis on the IPL Dataset.
  • Formulas and Functions : Use various Formulas and Functions to have an understanding on what happened during the matches and the perform an overall analysis.
  • Aggregate Functions: Used for summarizing data, calculating totals, averages, counts, etc., essential for deriving insights from large datasets.
  • Pivot Table and Charts: Get the Pivot Table to summarize the data for easier understanding.
  • Automation: Perform Automation using functions so users can get the details that are requested without having the need too look at the data.
  • Sorting and Filtering: Filtered and Sorted Data based on requirements for chart.
  • Publish: Publishing Charts to display the information to the viewers in a HTML page.
  • Web Scraping: Exported Data from IPL Website to get the names of the team of the players of the match for the year 2008.

Exploration on City

  • The games were held in 32 cities between the year 2008 - 2020.
  • The top 10 cities held 74.51%.
  • A total of 816 games were held.
  • There are 13 missing values in the city column.
  • 13 Games which were held in Sharjah Cricket Stadium and Dubai International Cricket Stadium which are in UAE, their cities were not listed.

Exploration on Years


  • The highest number of matches were held in 2013 with a number of 76
  • The lowest number of matches were held in 2009 with a number of 57
  • On Average, There were 63 matches every year approximately.

Exploration on Player of the Match

  • 234 players were recognized as the player of the match.
  • No Players received player of the match award in all the years.
  • We could see some players received the award continously every year without any gap from the year they started receiving.
  • RG Sharma received from 2009-2020 except 2008.
  • SK Raina received from 2008-2017 except from 2018-2020.
  • MS Dhoni received from 2008-2019 except 2020.

Info! Only data for 2008 is added for the player of the match.

Exploration on Toss Winner

  • Mumbai Indians, Kolkata Knight Riders, Chennai Super Kings, Kings XI Punjab, Rajastan Royals all have above 10% of the winning percentage.
  • Based on what we know, we need to compare with the Winning Percentage to determine if toss winning helps us with Winning the matches.

Info! We have considered the overall percentage between the years for the toss winners.

Exploration on Match Winners

  • Mumbai Indians, Kolkata Knight Riders, Chennai Super Kings, Kings XI Punjab, Rajastan Royals all had a higher count of wins.
  • There are also 4 NA in the count, which could mean both the teams did not win.
  • We need to further invetigate what percentage of winning the toss contributes to winning the match.

Connection between Toss and Match winning

  • We could see that the overall percentage is 51.23% which concludes that the toss does not contribute much to winning.

Info! We have taken the year 2020 for investigating .

  • We could see that the percentage in 2020 is 41.67% which is less than half so mostly results in loss.

Conclusion

  • We could see that the matches were held in stadiums within main cities like Mumbai, Kolkata, Delhi, Bangalore and Hyderabad.
  • Matches only between 60-70 for a year.
  • Toss winning and Match winning do not correlate and there is not that much causation.
